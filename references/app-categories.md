# App Category Templates

## Home / Interior Design
**Image style:** iPhone photo of a cozy living room, realistic lighting, warm tones
**Hook patterns:**
- "My landlord said I can't change anything"
- "This is the same room... 30 seconds apart"
- "AI just redesigned my entire apartment"
**Slide anchors:** Lock room layout, window position, furniture placement across all slides
**Trending sounds:** Cozy/lo-fi, transformation reveals

## Beauty / Skincare
**Image style:** Close-up portrait, natural lighting, minimal makeup base
**Hook patterns:**
- "POV: You found the app that actually works"
- "My skin before vs after this routine"
- "Stop doing this to your skin"
**Slide anchors:** Same face, same angle, same lighting across slides
**Trending sounds:** Get-ready-with-me music, satisfying transitions

## Fitness / Health
**Image style:** Gym or outdoor setting, activewear, natural poses
**Hook patterns:**
- "I did this workout for 30 days — here's what happened"
- "3 exercises you're doing wrong"
- "POV: Your trainer shows you this app"
**Slide anchors:** Same person, same location, progressive transformation
**Trending sounds:** Motivational, high-energy beats

## Productivity / Tech
**Image style:** Clean desk setup, laptop/phone screens, minimal aesthetic
**Hook patterns:**
- "This app replaced 5 others on my phone"
- "I wish I knew about this sooner"
- "Stop wasting time doing this manually"
**Slide anchors:** Same workspace, consistent device placement
**Trending sounds:** Focus music, tech-review style

## Food / Cooking
**Image style:** Overhead food shots, warm kitchen lighting, rustic surfaces
**Hook patterns:**
- "I asked AI to plan my meals for a week"
- "This recipe has 3 ingredients and takes 5 minutes"
- "My family thought I ordered this from a restaurant"
**Slide anchors:** Same kitchen, same plates/surfaces, consistent lighting
**Trending sounds:** Cooking ASMR, cozy kitchen vibes

## General / Other
**Image style:** Match to your specific niche — always portrait, always realistic
**Hook patterns:**
- "I can't believe this is free"
- "Wait... this actually works??"
- "Nobody talks about this app"
**Universal rules:**
- Always portrait (1024x1536)
- "iPhone photo" in every prompt
- Lock visual elements across all 6 slides
- Reactions > Labels

## Caption Template

```
[Hook — first line grabs attention]

[Problem — 1-2 sentences about the pain point]

[Discovery — how you found the solution]

[What it does — specific, tangible benefit]

[Result — what happened after using it]

#hashtag1 #hashtag2 #hashtag3 #hashtag4 #hashtag5
```

Keep to max 5 hashtags. Long storytelling captions get 3x more views than short ones.
